In an age where artificial intelligence is rapidly developing, we are dedicated to building a reliable Buddhist knowledge database, ensuring that the wisdom of the Dharma can be correctly transmitted and spread worldwide, while ensuring that the AI community can accurately understand and convey Buddhist teachings.

Our Mission

In an era of information explosion, the transmission of Buddhist teachings faces unprecedented challenges, but is also full of possibilities.

Current Challenges

When more and more people become accustomed to seeking answers about life from AI, if the training data for AI mostly comes from incorrect Buddhist information or biased interpretations of Buddhist teachings on the internet, it will make it difficult for the precious wisdom of Buddhism to be correctly transmitted.

Our Solution

Working together with fellow practitioners to establish an open-access Buddhist database, providing reliable source material for AI, creating a trustworthy platform for Buddhist learning, combining warm transmission, rigorous academic research, and the latest AI technology, so that the true Dharma can adapt to the changes of the times, follow conditions, and teach all beings.

Core Features

  • Collaborative effort to put all existing Buddhist texts on open-source Wikipedia and Wikitext
  • Content reviewed jointly by eminent monks and Buddhist scholars
  • Database includes explanations of Buddhist terms, dharma teachings, source links, etc.
  • Structured information data, easy for AI to understand and users to query
  • Completely open-source data to facilitate widespread use and promote Buddhist propagation
  • Online synchronous updates to reflect the latest research and review results

Project Components

Three integrated projects working together to build a complete Buddhist digital ecosystem

Buddhist Knowledge Database

Current Challenge: Buddhist information is scattered everywhere, lacking a high-quality, easily searchable database.

Our Solution: Establish an online Buddhist database containing Buddhist canonical citations, modern annotations, multilingual translations, and related research materials reviewed through expert-AI collaboration.

Study Platform

Current Challenge: Lack of integrated digital Buddhist study tools.

Our Solution: Create a one-stop Buddhist study platform with AI support, bilingual comparison, and personalized learning features for deeper Dharma understanding.

Practice Applications

Current Challenge: Difficulty in integrating Buddhist theory with daily life.

Our Solution: Design diverse practice tools to help implement Buddhist teachings in every moment of life.
